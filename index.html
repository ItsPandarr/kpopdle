How to play

Guess the hidden K-pop group or idol. Each guess shows how its attributes compare to the answer.

  • ✓exact match
  • ◐partial match (e.g. same parent label)
  • ▲answer's value is higher than your guess
  • ▼answer's value is lower than your guess
  • ✗no match

Daily gives one shared puzzle per day with a guess limit (Easy 6 · Medium 8 · Hard 10). Endless is practice — no cap, fresh target each round.

Hints reveal one attribute at a time. Cost decays as you guess; floor is +1. The button shows which attribute it would reveal.

Settings (the gear icon) holds theme, colorblind mode, calm-distractions mode, and detective mode (hides candidates that can't be the answer).

Theme
Colorblind mode

Swaps red/green for a deuteranopia-safe palette. Symbols (✓ ◐ ✗ ▲ ▼) are always shown.

Calm mode

Stops animations, shimmering text, and the drifting background. Confetti and entrance flips are disabled.

Detective mode

Hides candidates that can't be the answer given clues so far (e.g. wrong company, debuts outside the inferred range). Takes effect on the next puzzle.
